Welcome

Welcome to 'Zone'. In a modern day city 6 friends (age 19/20) meet up for the first day of school. It's been a nice vacation for all and they are looking forward to seeing each other again.

About

Zone is an RPG using Mekton Zeta© rules

It takes place in a large city in "kinda" present day. (no Mecha, no Roadstrikers, no Spaceships)

The main player characters are all 19 - 20 year and have been friends for a long time. They are all students of a large university (around 50.000 students).
